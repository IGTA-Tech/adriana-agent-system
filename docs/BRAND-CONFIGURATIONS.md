## Post-Call Analysis Fields

All agents capture:
- `caller_name` - Full name of the caller
- `caller_email` - Email address if provided
- `inquiry_topic` - What they are calling about
- `caller_type` - new_lead | existing_client | vendor | other
- `follow_up_needed` - Whether someone needs to call back
- `urgency` - urgent | normal | low
- `summary` - Brief summary of the conversation
